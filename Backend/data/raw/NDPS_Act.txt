NARCOTIC DRUGS AND PSYCHOTROPIC SUBSTANCES ACT, 1985 (NDPS) (Selected Sections)

Section 8. Prohibition of certain operations.
No person shall-
(a) cultivate any coca plant or gather any portion of coca plant;
(b) cultivate the opium poppy or any cannabis plant;
(c) produce, manufacture, possess, sell, purchase, transport, warehouse, use, consume, import inter-State, export inter-State, import into India, export from India or tranship any narcotic drug or psychotropic substance,
except for medical or scientific purposes...

Section 20. Punishment for contravention in relation to cannabis plant and cannabis.
Whoever, in contravention of any provision of this Act... cultivates any cannabis plant... produces, manufactures, possesses, sells... cannabis, shall be punishable,-
(b) where the contravention relates to Ganja or cultivation of cannabis plant... with rigorous imprisonment for a term which may extend to ten years...

Section 21. Punishment for contravention in relation to manufactured drugs and preparations.
Punishment varies based on "small quantity", "commercial quantity" or intermediate quantity.

Section 27. Punishment for consumption of any narcotic drug or psychotropic substance.
